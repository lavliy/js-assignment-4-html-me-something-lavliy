Introduction

"Doodling is an instant art form drawing, which gained popularity in the mordern age as creative outlet for mind relaxation. Here Im going to share my person experience of how I started doodling and how it helped to ease out my mind."

Joy of Doodling

"I started doodling to ease out my pressure during my days of Covid quarantitne. I was out of my mind within the four walls of the hospital, that drove me crazy so I decided to do something to stay normal. With my pocket journal and a green pen in hand I started my doodling Journey. I started to motivate myself first so, draw a simple doodle to say my "beBrave"."

Finding inspiration

"People around me are my sole inspiration, things happening in them made me to draw. I convey my regards with my doodles. The emotions expressed my them accelerated me to draw more."

Form of communication

"Every art form is a creative way of expressing our emotions to people. In that way sometimes doodles help us to communicate to the world how we feel. When my best friend miserable passed away I was out of words to share my grief, I was totally shattered, drawing gave me a way to mourn for him."

Benefits of Doodling

"Doodling not just give us a way to vent out our emotions. It enhances our concentration and focus towards things we do. Drawing helps us improve our fine motor skills."

Conclusion

"Doodling has been a transformative force in my life, shaping my creativity, self-expression, and overall well-being. Through doodling, I have discovered a profound sense of joy and liberation. It has become a means to explore my imagination, communicate my thoughts and emotions, and find solace in moments of stress"
